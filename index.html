About

I am a PhD student working at the AI4Energy research group in IDLab, Ghent university —imec. My research focuses on the application of advanced machine learning algorithms for realizing residential demand response solutions. This includes studying about control paradigms such as reinforcement learning as well as novel forecasting techniques such as Temporal Fusion Transformers.

Research

My current research includes model-free reinforcement learning, physics informed neural network and other specialized neural architectures for sample-efficient and interpretable controller design.
